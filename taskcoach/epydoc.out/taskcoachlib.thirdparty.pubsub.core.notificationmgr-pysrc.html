  2  Manages notifications by pubsub. Pubsub notifies this manager once for 
  3  every type of notification. If the corresponding notification flag is 
  4  True, all registered notification handlers are called (order undefined) 
  5  via the flag's associated method (handler.sendMessage for sendMessage 
  6  notification, etc). 
  7
